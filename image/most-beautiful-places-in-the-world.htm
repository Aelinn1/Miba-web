Top Beautiful Places in the World You Must Visit This Year

Earth is filled with a number of majestic surprises and surreal places with awe-inspiring beauty. There is so much to see that one lifetime won’t be enough to experience all the beautiful places in the world. Every country offers some beautiful places in the world worth seeing and the time required to absorb all the beauty is painfully long.

A small list is not enough to explore all the best places to travel in the world but with some help, you can check a few places out of your bucket list for sure. Whether you want to visit the UK or wish to explore the natural scenic surprises of Chile. You can take a walk through the gorgeous markets of Istanbul or take a hot air balloon ride in Began, the beauty around these best places in the world. All you need to do is to stay patient and know the best way to experience and welcome surprises coming from the beautiful places in the world as you travel.

List of Beautiful Places in The World

The Fairy Pools on the Isle of Skye – Scotland

Scotland is filled with a number of beautiful places in the world. Some of the best places in the world include the Fairy Pools on the Isle of Skye. This romantic and inspiring place has made many writers and poets spend their time and create their masterpieces. If you wish to indulge in wild swimming, jump into the icy cold waters of Fairy Pools of Scotland. Located at the foot of the Black Cuillins near Glenbrittle, Fairy Pools’ crystal blue waters are a mind-numbing delight for nature and adventure lovers. This part of Europe has a unique geography. The natural landscapes and scenic wonders get it counted among the best places in the world for the honeymooners, adventurers and photographers.

Loch Ness, Loch Lomond and Edinburgh Castle are some of the most popular tourist destinations in the world located here. With so many Amazing places in the world, how can one not spend some time in Scotland?

Rainbow mountains, China

Rainbow Mountains are one of the many beautiful places in the world that China boasts about. These gorgeous mountains look like a painting. Artists can take a great inspiration from the Rainbow Mountains in China – earth’s natural paint palette. National Geographic counts Rainbow Mountains as one of top geographical wonders of the world. Many travel guidebooks have listed it as must visit amazing places in the world because of the sheer beauty.

Located within the Zhangye Danxia Landform Geological Park, Rainbow Mountains mimic a rainbow painted over the rolling mountains. This is one of the beautiful places in the world you should consider visiting.

The site was named a UNESCO world heritage site in 2009. Not only the Chinese but also the international tourists travel all the way to China to experience the beauty of the one of the best places in the world that this mountain is.

Railay Beach, Thailand

Located in a remote paradise, Railay beach is one of the many beautiful places that makes Thailand counted among the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Railay Beach is the pride of Thailand tourism.

Railay Beach is considered one of the mesmerising and beautiful places in the world because of its natural placement. Isolated by the mainland by limestone cliffs and surrounded by dense jungle, Railay beach’s beauty is unmatchable to any other destination in Thailand. No wonder, Railay Beach is so stunning and laidback, making it counted among the beautiful locations in the world.

Located between the city of Krabi and Ao Nang, Railay is divided into two parts, the West and the East. The Western part is more popular and has better beach with crystalline turquoise waters to charm your senses. Because of so many scenic surprises, this unique Thailand destination is listed among the wonderful places in the world.

Northern Lights, Iceland

Iceland, in general, comes in the list of top three most beautiful places in the world. Its natural setting, mountains, geysers are already amazing and scenic. There are so many beautiful places in Iceland but one reason for which everyone would count this country among best places to travel in the world will be its northern lights.

Northern Light is a spectacular natural phenomenon that takes place in Iceland during winter months. Also known as Aurora Borealis, it occurs due to the interaction of the particles of the sun with the upper atmosphere near the North Pole. It’s one of those out of the world experiences that lingers on in your memory for a lifetime.

Iceland tour packages comprise of Northern light tours.

Marble Caverns of Carrera Lake – Chile

From Dramatic Atacama Desert in the North to mesmerising fiords in the South, Chile has everything that would make it counted among amazing places in the world. Chile is one of the most diverse countries in the terms of geography. Because of this, the country hides many beautiful locations in the world around its many corners.

Marble Caverns of Carrera Lake in Chile are the most beautiful caves in the world. This spectacular cave network is an intricate system of water-filled caverns sitting gracefully on the General Carrera lake in Chile’s Patagonia.

The entire lake covers a surface of 1850 square kilometers. Out of this huge area 97p sq km is inside Chile. The geographical beauty and a piece of natural wonder is one of the most beautiful places in the world. Some other beautiful places in Chile Include the world-famous Easter Island and Torres Del Paine national park.

Galápagos Islands

Galapagos Islands is a destination for many researches and wildlife enthusiasts who come here to understand the evolutionary cycle. The natural beauty and scenic diversity is the reason why Republic of Ecuador counts is among its most beautiful places. The beautiful Galapagos Islands is an isolated group of volcanic islands that form a fragile ecosystem, showcasing biodiversity in a unique way. Situated in the Pacific Ocean some 1,000 km from the South American continent, Galapagos Islands is a UNESCO world heritage site.

Traveling through Galapagos Islands is a once in a lifetime opportunity for everyone exploring the natural wonders across the best tourist places in world. For any nature enthusiast, these volcanic islands are the best places to visit in the world. The many beautiful places here include Baltra, Santa Cruz Island and Genovese Island.

Cappadocia, Turkey

Credits – www.popsugar.com

The pristine wonders of Turkey include many beautiful places like Istanbul, Pool of Abraham and Cappadocia. Among these places, there are so many cultural surprises hidden everywhere which are worth exploring and spending your time. As one of the most amazing tourists places in the world, Cappadocia attracts many visitors every year.

The honeycombed hills and towering boulders of Cappadocia may make you feel that you have landed on Mars. A hot air balloon ride here acquaints you with the fantastical topography of Cappadocia in Turkey. The sunset turns Cappadocia into a painting, the pictures itself prove why it is counted among amazing places in the world.

Turkey tour packages will bring you up close to the beauty of Cappadocia.

Ashikaga Flower Park: Ashikaga, Japan

Ashikaga Flower Park is one of the best places to view fuji flowers. The colourful blanket makes the place look like a fairyland. If the beauty is so impeccable, why won’t anyone include it in their list of best places to visit in the world? It features a plethora of blue, white and pink fuji. The winter illumination display in the park is a sight to behold.

Japan has so many wonders that are counted among one of the most amazing places in the world. The country’s cherry blossom festival has made Japan rise in the ranks of the must visit tourist places in world.

Milford Sound, New Zealand

New Zealand has a number of best places to visit in the world. The natural and scenic surprises make New Zealand look like a fairyland. The culture and the heritage that New Zealand has to show is the reason why many tourist places in world are still untouched and has maintained the natural aura.

According to Rudyard Kipling, Milford Sound is the eighth wonder of the world. And if you pay a visit to this region, you will have all the reasons to believe this. Dotted with breathtaking landscapes and cliffs rising from fjords, Milford Sound was formed by glaciers during the ice age. Today, this place is among favourite tourist destinations in the world for anyone who wishes to take a peek into the geographical wonders that time can create.

Four Seasons, Bora Bora

Credits – www.yexplore.travel

Four Seasons, Bora Bora is on the bucket lists of many. Honeymooners and nature lovers want to visit this island and it has been mentioned as one of the best places to visit in the world by many travel enthusiasts. Recently awarded the title of “the best island in the world’, Four seasons in Bora Bora is one of the most beautiful places in the world. A luxury holiday in Bora Bora is a perfect place to celebrate your new nuptial.

Four Seasons, Bora Bora talks about luxury from every angle. If you wish to have a pampering experience while traveling to one of the beautiful locations in the world, then Bora Bora is where you should be.

Plitvice Lakes National Park, Croatia

Credits – www.tarvel49.com

When 16 crystalline lakes tumble into each other to form a series of cascading waterfalls, it’s a sight to behold. This national park and one of the most beautiful locations in the world is home to numerous scenic lakes, caves and waterfalls. This national park has a world hidden inside. Because of such wonders it is counted among the amazing places in the world. Plitvice Lakes National Park in Croatia is one of the most beautiful geological wonders in the world.

Apart from this national park, Croatia has copious amount of islands worth exploring. The sun, the sand and the beaches have a heaven for the traveller and known for being amazing places in the world worth spending some time at. Dugi Otok is one of the largest islands in Croatia and is easily accessible, so when you are done with basking in the beauty of the Plitvice Lakes National Park, you can take out some time and explore this island. The high cliffs and the rugged coastline makes Dungi Otok one of the most wonderful places in the world.

Santorini, Greece

Greece is a paradise for the honeymooners. Filled with gorgeous landscapes, buildings and lovely plazas, Greece is a country with several best places to visit in the world. Tell anyone about your trip to Greece and the first name they’ll suggest will be Santorini. Counted among marvellous tourist destinations in the world with amazing heritage and culture, Santorini is worth considering for your honeymoon trip.

Santorini in Greece is gaining a lot of popularity among travellers. With a sea-drowned caldera as the main feature and beautiful smaller islands in the background, Santorini surely is one of the most beautiful places in the world.

Santorini is always the main destination in most Europe Holiday packages. With multiple wonders like Nafplio, Corfu and ancient Knossos, Greece has mind boggling selection of best places in the world spending some time at.

Munnar, India

Kerala in India is where Munnar is located. Surrounded by amazing and gorgeous Western Ghats, the tea gardens of Munnar make it one of the tourist places in world worth visiting. There is no other best places to travel in the world unlike the scenic paradise called Munnar.

The beautiful tea plantations on the rolling mountains of Munnar attracts millions of tourists every year. A ride on the snaking roads of Munnar is a delightful experience. Munnar is always a part of Kerala holiday packages.

Kerala has a number of destinations of tourist interest that are worth combining with your trip to Munnar. You can experience the serenity of the Alleppey backwaters or enjoy the art districts of Kochi. Kerala is clustered with many wonderful places in the world and everything has something unique and worth learning about.

With more than 80 marble domes on the roof line sitting atop more than 1000 pillars, Sheikh Zayed Mosque in Abu Dhabi is an important part of Dubai tourism. As a man-made wonder, this mosque has no match in the world. This is why it is so popular and counted as must visit tourist places in world. This snow-white mosque rising majestically from the perfectly manicured gardens definitely hits the list of most beautiful mosques in the world. Also known as “Garden of Europe”, Keukenhof Park from Amsterdam features millions of vibrant tulips, as well as other springtime blooms like bluebells, daffodils, and hyacinths. The Great Barrier Reef, Australia

Lavender fields: Provence, France

The country that gets to redefine love and romance will obviously be among tourist places in world worth traveling. The fragrance and the beauty of Lavender Fields in Provence is sure to inspire the artist in you. It’s a quaint place where one can enjoy tranquillity far away from maddening crowds. Pmaukalle, Turkey

Another gorgeous place and popular tourist destinations in the world is from Turkey. The white travertine terraces, limpid pools and mighty waterfalls make Pamukalle the most frequented attraction of Turkey. Meaning ‘Cotton Castle’ in Turkish, Pamukkale features well-preserved ruins of the Greek-Roman city of Hierapolis. Pamukkale literally translates to cotton castle. The famous spa of Pamukkale has been around since the Roman Era. This unique attraction is the reason it is counted among best places to travel in the world.

Angkor Wat, Cambodia


As one of the popular tourist places in world, Cambodian Buddhist Temples draw a huge crowd. Angkor Wat is the most beautiful thing in Cambodia. Featuring a series of ancient temples, Angkor Wat gives you a glimpse of contemporary Cambodia ruled by the mighty Khmer Empire. In the backdrop of sunset and sunrise, this temple becomes a majestic piece of beauty. Many travel guides have mentioned it as a compulsory and must visit tourist destinations in the world.

Salar de Uyuni, Bolivia


When it comes to the popular tourist destinations in the world for history and archaeology enthusiasts, Salar de Uyuni is a place that draws many from all over the globe. Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia is the remains of a prehistoric lake that went dry, leaving behind a massive landscape of bright-white salt, rock formations and cacti-studded islands.

White Haven Beach, Australia

There are so many best places in the world but Australia’s destinations are on a whole new level. The nature, the beaches, the marine and wildlife make Australia counted among the beautiful locations in the world worth traveling and exploring. White haven is among the most beautiful beaches in the world, for it comprises of white silica sand in a combination of turquoise waters, making it the purest beach in the world. An aerial view from a helicopter of the beach is sure to delight the surrounding aura.

Australia tourism has a lot of natural wonders to offers.

Mendenhall Ice Caves, Juneau, Alaska

Mendenhall Ice Caves are partially hollow glaciers with shimmering blue ceilings. The entire setting looks like a picture from a fantasy novel. No wonder it is listed as one of the most wonderful tourist destinations in the world. Trekking on the glacier will acquaint you with ice caves, dramatic crevasses and interesting glacial formations. It surely is one of the most beautiful places in the world.

One can also travel to Glacier Bay National park and Denali National Park and feel the beauty of the many best places in the world, hidden in every corner. Among the wonderful places in the world that are popular for Northern Lights, Alaska is considered among one of the best to see this unique phenomenon.

Lake Hillier, Australia

While Australia comprises of some of the most beautiful water bodies, the lakes here are worth visiting too. As one of the beautiful locations in the world, Lake Hillier attracts many because of its unique landscape. Also known as Pink Lake, Lake Hillier in Australia delights your eye with its bubblegum pink colour.

The unique contrast between the mellow pink of the lake and the blue of the ocean is something rare and breath-taking. The reason why this lake is pink colored is because of Dunaliella salina, a species of halophilic micro-algae, which resides in this lake. No wonder, the unique beauty that this lake show gets it counted among the wonderful places in the world.

Lake Louise, Canada

image source

Like any other place on this list, Lake Louise in Canada is also one of the most Beautiful Places in the World. Every season here brings out something more awe-inspiring. Come summers, the lake shimmers with its turquoise waters. Falls bring out the enchanting golden hue in the trees speckled all around, while the winters leave you speechless when the lake freezes the whole area gets covered by a thick blanket of mystifying fog and pure white snow. And lastly, with the arrival of spring, the whole region adopts a cheerful vibe with lush greenery all around. No matter which season you visit it, it will seduce you with its timeless beauty nonetheless. Just remember to take plenty of pictures, especially in the early hours of the day when its allure is at its peak.

Mount Fuji can be best observed and pictured from Fuji Five Lakes Region and in the months of October to February when the whole area looks stunning, covered in snow.

Namib-Naukluft National Park, Namibia

image source
